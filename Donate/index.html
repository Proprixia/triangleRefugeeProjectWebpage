How You Can Help

There are two ways to help:

  • Donate new or used school supplies to one of our collection locations. A full list of locations is available to the right. (Note: List will be available when locations list is finalized.)
  • Order school supplies to our doorstep using our Amazon wishlist.

What Materials
Do We Need?

  • Backpacks
  • Lunchboxes
  • Water Bottles
  • Binders
  • Lined and Graphed Paper
  • Pencils
  • Colored Pencils
  • Glue Sticks
  • Crayons

DISCLAIMER: The Triangle Refugee Student Project is NOT affiliated with the Boy Scouts of America or any other scouting organization. The Triangle Refugee Student Project is a project of an individual boy scout, Andrew Lynch, who is organizing the project as a requirement for his Eagle Scout rank. 100% of donations benefit the United States Committee for Refugees and Immigrants of North Carolina, and by proxy local refugee students. Neither any Scouting organization nor Andrew Lynch will receive any financial enrichment from this project.
